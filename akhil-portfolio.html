About Me

I'm Akhil Kulal B, a Cybersecurity Engineer who bridges the gap between development velocity and security rigor. My journey started in DevOps, giving me a deep understanding of how modern infrastructure is built — and exactly where it breaks.

Today, I specialize in AWS cloud security, helping organizations architect environments that are secure by design — not bolted on after the fact. I work across the full stack of compliance: ISO 27001, ISO 42001, HIPAA, SOC 2, and Cyber Essentials.

My philosophy is simple: security is a continuous process, not a project. I'm driven by technical excellence, proactive vulnerability management, and the belief that every company — regardless of size — deserves enterprise-grade protection.

Outside the terminal, I stay curious. Follow my journey on Instagram @akhil_bk_ and connect professionally on LinkedIn.
